Per Diem Days

Days away from gateway. Remember sure to exclude paid holidays if you are estimating every duty day to be a day away from the gateway. e.g On a 200 day contract with 22 days of holiday, where all duty days are away from your gateway, you would get 178 per diems.

Block Hours

Total block hours you think you will fly this year. Round down to the nearest hour.

CPR Bonus Year

There are profit shares for profitable years, paid out based on flight time. Toggle on if you expect SafeJets to be profitable this year.

Cabin Crew Fleet

Toggle on if your fleet carries Cabin Crew, for the purpose of Tour Based Pay calculations (TBP). TBP is toggled on if you select a contract higher than 200 days.

APU Equipped Fleet

Select on if your aircraft is equipped with an APU, for the purpose of Tour Based Pay calculations (TBP). TBP is toggled on if you select a contract higher than 200 days.

Contracted Days

Select the duty days outlined in your contract. The normal contract is 200 days of duty.

Extended Days (NED & HED)

Extended are extra days that you fly on tour. This occurs whenever SafeJets wants you to exceed a specified number of duty hours during a tour. Operations will ask you permission to extend. If you accept, you will receive additonal compensation for the additional duty.

Normal Extended Day

If your extended day will not make you exceed your quarterly contracted days (49/51/51/49 for the standard 200 days contract), you will receive a normal extended day rate.

High Extended Day

If your extended day will make you exceed your quarterly contracted days (49/51/51/49 for the standard 200 days contract), you will receive a high extended day rate.

Taxman's Chunk

It's impossible to accommodate for every country's and every individual's tax situation. This is a simple single tax percentage removal. There is no tax allowance, national insurance etc calculator. It's a for a simple guesstimation.
