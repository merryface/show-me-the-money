Per Diem Days

Days away from gateway. Remember to exclude paid holidays if you are estimating every duty day to be a day away from the gateway. e.g On a 200 day contract with 22 days of holiday, where all duty days are away from your gateway, you would get 178 per diems. Per diems are at 70 euros per day.

Block Hours

Total block hours you think you will fly this year. Round down to the nearest hour. The block hours value is used for the CPR calculation.

CPR Bonus Year

CPR is payable whether the company is profitable or not. Toggle on if you want to see what the total salary will be with CPR. The rates of CPR Productivity Reward shall be payable for annual Corrected Assigned Block Hours. The payment is triggered once the tour Corrected Assigned Block Hour threshold is reached and applies per block hour in excess of the threshold. For the purpose of TBP, Tour duration only considers flying related duty codes.

Cabin Crew Fleet

Toggle on if your fleet carries Cabin Crew, for the purpose of Tour Based Pay calculations (TBP). TBP is toggled on if you select a contract higher than 200 days.

APU Equipped Fleet

Select on if your aircraft is equipped with an APU, for the purpose of Tour Based Pay calculations (TBP). TBP is toggled on if you select a contract higher than 200 days. If you select a TBP contract (204 and more), your base salary will be adjusted accordingly. It will be an increase of 2.5% for every 4 extra duty days.

Contracted Days

Select the duty days outlined in your contract. The normal contract is 200 days of duty.

Extended Days (NED & HED)

Extended are extra days that you fly on tour. This occurs whenever SafeJets wants you to exceed a specified number of duty hours during a tour. Operations will ask you permission to extend. If you accept, you will receive additonal compensation for the additional duty. The app currently applies the following daily rates until the current official values are identified.

First Officer Captain
Normal Extended Day 655 € 835 €
High Extended Day 860 € 1045 €

Normal Extended Day

If your extended day will not make you exceed your quarterly contracted days (49/51/51/49 for the standard 200 days contract), you will receive a normal extended day rate.

High Extended Day

If your extended day will make you exceed your quarterly contracted days (49/51/51/49 for the standard 200 days contract), you will receive a high extended day rate.

Late Home Days

Late Home returns are when a duty is extended to beyond midnight on the last rostered duty day, but does not contain any flight duty. In this case 50% of NED is paid.

Vacation Sell off

Crew can sell vacation days in certain circumstances for the value of 0.5% of their base values. Enter the number of vacation days sold.

Taxman's chunk

It's impossible to accommodate for every country's and every individual's tax situation. This is a simple single tax percentage removal. There is no tax allowance, national insurance etc calculator. It's for a simple guesstimation.
